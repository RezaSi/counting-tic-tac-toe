The Goal

Tic-tac-toe is a turn-based game, where the objective is to get three in a row, but in this game, player who have greater count of three in a row(vertically, horizontally or diagonally), wins the game.

  Rules

The game is played on a 9x9 grid. You must output the coordinate of the cell you want to mark. The player player who have greater count of three in a row(vertically, horizontally or diagonally), wins the game.

  Game Input

Input for one game turn
Line 1: 2 space separated integers opponentRow and opponentCol, the opponent's last action (-1 -1 for the first turn).
Line 2: the number of valid actions for this turn, validActionCount.
Next validActionCount lines: 2 space separated integers row and col, the coordinates you're allowed to play at.
Output for one game turn
Line 1: 2 space separated integers row and col.
